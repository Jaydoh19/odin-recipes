French Toast

Home

Description

French toast is bread soaked in a mixture of eggs, milk, and spices, then pan-fried\ until golden. It’s usually served hot with toppings like syrup, fruit, or powdered sugar .

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 12 thick slices bread

Steps

  1. Prepare Batter: Whisk flour and milk, then add eggs, sugar, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt until smooth.
  2. Preheat Pan: Lightly oil a griddle or frying pan and heat over medium.
  3. Soak Bread: Dip bread slices into the batter until fully soaked.
  4. Cook: Fry soaked bread until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve: Serve hot and enjoy.
