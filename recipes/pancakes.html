Buttermilk Pancakes

Home

Description

Buttermilk pancakes are fluffy, golden breakfast classics with a slight tang from buttermilk, perfect for soaking up butter and syrup.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups buttermilk
  • ½ cup milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted

Steps

  1. Mix Dry Ingredients: Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl
  2. Mix Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, beat together buttermilk, milk, eggs, and melted butter.
  3. Preheat Griddle: Heat a lightly oiled griddle over medium-high heat until water sizzles on it.
  4. Combine Mixtures: Pour wet ingredients into dry and stir until just blended (batter should be slightly lumpy).
  5. Cook Pancakes: Pour about ½ cup batter per pancake onto the griddle. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and brown the other side.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
